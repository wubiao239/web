Host: Good morning, everyone. I’m glad to see you again. About the feldspar, maybe somebody are not strange. Then, how about its crushing production plant and the machine? Do you know clearly? Today, we are honored to invite the expert—Mr. Wang to give us a detailed introduction about feldspar crushing production plant and the processing equipment. Welcome Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: Hello, everyone. I’m happy to take part in the interview. Hope I will give you a satisfied introduction.

Host: Welcome your coming again, Mr. Wang! The feldspar is one of the most common mineral. Can you first introduce its general situation for us?

Mr. Wang: OK. Feldspar is raw materials of ceramic and glass industry. Some may also be used as a gemstone material. The application field of feldspar is wide. In the glass industry, feldspar can be used as raw materials for all kinds of glass products, reducing the glass melting temperature. In the ceramic industry, it is the main raw material for ceramic, enamel, porcelain blank and glaze. Feldspar is also one of the raw materials for the production of white cement. In addition, feldspar has widely use as filler in papermaking, refractory materials, machinery manufacturing, paint, wire and other industrial production.

Host: Since its role is so important, its production line must be more, doesn’t it?

Mr. Wang: Yes, it doesn’t. There are many different crushing machine combinations. The common crushing machine comprises jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, vibrating feeder, elevator, hopper, vibrating screen and belt conveyor. Here, I take the jaw crusher and impact crusher as an example to relate the crushing plant. Jaw crusher is as the first crushing machine, and impact crusher is as the second crushing machine. The feldspar material will enter into jaw crusher and impact crusher for the crushing process. Then, it’s turn to be screened. Under the action of vibrating screen, we can get the finished product.

Host: This is a wonderful introduction. Thanks Mr. Wang to take part in the interview.

Mr. Wang: You are welcome. This is also my honor.
