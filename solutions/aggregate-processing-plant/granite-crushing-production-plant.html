Granite is the important decorative material. Its application is so many. So, its processing methods are also diverse. No matter the crushing production, or the grinding production, it both needs the crushing equipment. So we can think that the crushing production plant is very important for granite. Today, let’s together discuss one of the common crushing plants. The following is the detailed introduction.

Granite Application

Granite is the main component of the continental crust. It has a vital role in our daily life. The main component of granite is feldspar and quartz. It is an excellent construction aggregate Granite is a rock with very wide distribution, and many countries have produced granite in the world. Because the granite has high hardness and abrasion resistance, besides being used for advanced building decoration engineering and the ground outside the hall, it is also the first choice for open carving wood. So we can say that granite is dressed up with our life.

The Common Crushing Equipment

The crushing production plant must need the crusher. But the crushers have many models, which one we should choose? This need to according to your actual production demand, for example, the production capacity, material property. As long as meet the requirements, you can choose jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, and hammer crusher. According to the finished product size, you can chose only one stage crushing process or two stages crushing process.

The common crushing plant includes many machines, such as the jaw crusher, impact crusher, hopper, vibrating feeder, vibrating screen, elevator, and belts.

The Granite Crushing Production Plant

Here, we take jaw crusher and impact crusher as an example. Jaw crusher is as the first crushing machine and the impact crusher is as the second crushing machine. We choose the common two stages crushing process.

First, granite is transported to jaw crusher for the primary crushing by the vibrating feeder. Then, the materials with the suitable size will enter into impact crusher for the second crushing. At last, under the action of vibrating screen, we can get the suitable finished product. The other materials will enter into the crusher again. This is the common granite crushing production plant.
