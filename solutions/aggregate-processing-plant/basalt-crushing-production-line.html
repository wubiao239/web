Host: In the modern industries, the basalt has a very wide use. It is the precious raw material. With the increasing use rang, the basalt crushing production line is diverse. The crushing machine collocation is also different. Today, we are honored to invite the expert—Mr. Wang to give us a detailed introduction about basalt crushing production plant and the crushing equipment. Welcome Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: Hello, everyone. I’m happy to take part in the interview. Hope I will give you a satisfied answer.

Host: We know that the basalt has a wide application. So can you first introduce its development for us?

Mr. Wang: I’m glad. There is no doubt that basalt has a very wide application. It is not only limited in the construction industry. Basalt is a good material to produce "cast stone". It can form the hard and wear-resistant alloy steel. Basalt can play a "lubricant" effect in an advanced technique of cast steel. It can prolong the service life of cast film. Basalt can also be pumped into glass fiber. Basalt glass’s alkali resistance is better than the general glass, the same is true of high temperature resistant performance. So I believe that in the future, basalt’s role will be larger.

Host: With the increasing of basalt’s application, its processing production line is more and more. How about its crushing equipment?

Mr. Wang: The crushing equipment has many types, such as the jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, hammer crusher and so on. According to the different regions, basalt’s physical properties are different. We can also configure different equipment. In generally, we choose the jaw crusher as the first crushing machine. Cone crusher or impact crusher is the second crushing equipment. Of course, you can also choose three crushing stages.

Host: So how about the basalt crushing production line? Can you simply relate it?

Mr. Wang: Of course. When we select the crushing equipment, the crushing line is certain. First, the basalt is transported into jaw crusher for the first crushing process. After the first crushing, through the belt conveyor, basalt is transported to cone crushers for secondary crushing. Then, this basalt is transported to vibrating screen by belt conveyor. At last, we can get the suitable size.

Host: Thank you, Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: Not at all.
