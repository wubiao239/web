Limestone is the important construction material and industry material. With the development of foundation construction, the limestone’s application will be wider and wider. So its crushing production plant and the crushing equipment are very important. Now, let analyze its production line. The following is the common crushing equipment. Hope it will be helpful for you.

Limestone Application

Limestone is used the architectural decoration materials. In addition, cement is made up of limestone and clay under the high temperature. The glass is composed of limestone, quartz sand, soda ash and other mixed, by high temperature melting. The iron-making is used limestone as flux. Calcium carbide is limestone and coke in the furnace reaction process. Limestone can be used as desiccant and disinfectant. In agriculture, use the limestone to make lime sulphur, and Bordeaux mixture pesticide.

The Common Limestone Crushing Equipment

Limestone crushing machines have many different combinations. The common crushing production line machine mainly have jaw crusher, cone crusher, hopper, vibrating feeder, vibrating screen, and a number of belt conveyors. Sometimes, we also use the impact crusher as the secondary crushing equipment. Every machine has many types. According to the material property and production requirements, we can also choose the different types.

Limestone crushing production plant

First, the limestone is transported to the jaw crusher by the vibrating feeder. In the jaw crusher, limestone has the primary crushing process. Then, the suitable limestone will enter into the cone crusher for the second crushing. After the crushing process, this limestone is transported to the vibrating screen by belt conveyor. After screening, the limestone which meet the size requirement, become the finish product through the screen stencil. While the limestone which don’t meet the size requirement, will again enter into the cone crusher for crushing. The vibrating screen will screens the suitable size finished product.

Of course, limestone grinding production line is also frequently-used. It needs to use the grinding equipment, such as the ultrafine mill, trapezium mill, vertical mill and ball mill. But it also needs to have the crushing production plant. So the limestone crushing equipment is very important for its processing.
