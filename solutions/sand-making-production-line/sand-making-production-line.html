Sand production line is a dedicated production line of sand and stone production equipment. It is widely used in stone material factory, mine, metallurgy, building materials, highway, railway, water conservancy, chemical industry and other departments. The production capacity and product size can be combined and classified according to the different needs of users. The following is the detailed introduction about sand making line and mechanism sand equipment.

The Production Process Of Sand Making Line

Stone is evenly sent to the jaw crusher by vibrating feeder for the coarse crushing. After the coarse crushing, it will enter into the sand making machine for further broken by belt conveyor. Then, the fine crushing material is transported to the vibrating screen for screening process.

The materials which are suitable for the size requirement will be fed into the sand washing machine for washing. After cleaning, this material is becoming the finished product by the conveyor belt output.

The materials which are not suitable for the size requirement will return into the sand making machine, forming many closed loop. The particle size of finished products can be combined and classified according to the needs of users. If choose the dry production process, it can be equipped with thick powder separator and dust removal equipment.

Sand Production Equipment And The Structure

The Feeding system

The system is to transport the raw materials crusher and screen equipment. The common feeding process equipment is the vibration feeder or other types of feeding equipment. In the sand production line, the feeder equipment is responsible for the supply of stone.

The crushing system

The system is a complete set of equipment of the heart. It is the job of the all kinds of ore crushing raw materials into finished material required size of. A combination of sand production line can have more than one crusher composition. These broken mechanical properties vary, common to complete crushing stone.

Sieving and conveying system

In the sand stone composite production line, sand and stone is the need to separate. Sand and stone both need to deliver to their site. This process is generally used the vibrating screen or other screening mechanical device.
