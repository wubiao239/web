So today, we are honored to invite the expert—Mr. Wang to give us a detailed introduction about copper ore processing production plant and the equipment. Welcome Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: Hello, everyone. I’m happy to take part in the interview. Hope I will give you a satisfied answer.

Host: Copper is familiar to us. Around of us, there is its shadow. Maybe you can't think of it at first. It does not matter. We can listen to Mr. Wang to introduce it.

Mr. Wang: I’m glad. Copper is widely used in industry. It can be used in electrical, mechanical, vehicle, ship, building industry and civil appliances. At present, in the field of electrical and electronic industry, copper is mainly used in the manufacture of wire, communication cable and other products, such as motor, the rotor and electronic instruments, etc. Copper and copper alloy play an important role in computer chips, integrated circuits, transistors, printed circuit boards and other equipment device. In the construction industry, copper can be used for a variety of pipes, pipe fittings, decoration device etc.

Host: After listening to your introduction, I know it more deeply. With its increasing application, what do the processing production plant and equipment have any changes?

Mr. Wang: Due to the different production demand, we can use the different crusher, grinder and others. With the development of science and market need, copper production methods has more and more. The machines are diverse. The production line is also diverse. In the future, in order to meet our demand, it will be developing.

Host: So can you relate the crushing production plant and machines?

Mr. Wang: Of course. The crushing process is the essential for processing the copper ore. Copper ore processing equipment contains a lot of machines, for example jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, hammer crusher, and mobile crusher. We can choose the better crusher.
