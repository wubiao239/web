Host: Hello, everyone. We know that gypsum is an important industrial raw material. With the development of gypsum products industry, gypsum powder has become a scarce material. But it still has an important position in the national economy. About its processing production plant, there are a lot of different combinations. Today, we invite the expert from Shanghai MPM to relate gypsum processing production plant and the machine. Welcome!

Expert: Good afternoon. I’m happy to take part in the interview. Hope you have a nice time!

Host: Gypsum’s production methods are more and more. This means that its application is wider and wider, doesn’t it?

Expert: Yes. Gypsum’s application is very broad. In the building materials industry, along with the increasing of high-rise buildings, gypsum powder can not meet the market demand. In the glass production process, gypsum powder can be used as the cosolvent and purifying agent. In plastic, rubber, paint, asphalt, and asphalt felt industry production, it is used as the filler. It is the main raw material of concrete expansion agent and anti-cracking agent. In agriculture, it can improve the soil and adjust the PH value. With the rapid development of economy, the demand of architecture, building materials and other related industries increase sharply. Gypsum has aroused more and more attention, and has formed a new industry giant.

Host: Since its application is so big. Then, how about its processing production plant and machine? Is the type more?

Expert: Gypsum’s processing production plant has a lot. It usually needs to go through the crushing and grinding process. If it is only the crushing process, we only need to use the crushers. In general, we use the two stages crushing process. Jaw crusher is as the first crushing machine, and cone crusher or impact crusher is as the second crushing process. After the crushing process, it needs to be screened. Under the role of vibrating screen, we can get the finished gypsum product.

Host: How about the grinding production process?

Expert: The grinding production line is similar with the crushing line. We can use ultrafine mill, vertical mill, trapezium mill and ball mill.

Host: Thank you for your detailed introduction.

Expert: You are welcome.
