Gold Ore Processing Production Plant

Host: Gold ore is mined by artificial or mechanical method, which also contains other metal impurities. Gold is a used to create the actual mining and decorative works of precious metal. It is the precious mineral resource. Many people ask me about its processing production plant. So today, we invite the expert from Shanghai MPM to give us a detailed introduction. Welcome him!

Expert: Hello, everyone. I’m happy to share my study. Hope you will have a nice day.

Host: As far as I know, for processing gold ore, the crushing process is essential. So can you first introduce it and its crushing machine?

Expert: Of course. The crushing process is necessary. So we can first understand it. It can’t do without the crushers, such as jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher, hammer crusher and others. In general, we can choose two or three stages crushing process. Jaw crusher is usual the first crushing machine. Cone crusher or impact crusher is as the second crushing machine. In addition, we also need the vibrating screen, vibrating feeder, hopper, and belt conveyor. Of course, if you need the grinding process, we can also add the grinder. We can use ultrafine mill, trapezium mill, vertical mill, and ball mill.

Host: I hear that in the gold ore processing production plant, the beneficiation process is needed, isn’t it?

Expert: Yes. For gold ore, the beneficiation process is indispensable. The common beneficiation machine has the flotation machine, magnetic separation machine, spiral classify and so on. The two former are the most common used. We can also say that Gold ore dressing mainly includes gravity separation and flotation separation. Gravity separation method plays an important role in the gold production. Flotation is used widely for rock gold mine ore dressing method. In addition, there are the chemical processing method, and the heap leaching production process. With the development of science and technology, its production plant will more and more.

Host: This is a wonderful introduction. Thank you for your coming.

Expert: You are welcome. I’m also happy to take part in the interview.
