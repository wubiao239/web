Host: I believe we are not strange with the iron ore. It is the world's most common use metal, and is an important raw material of steel. Iron ore process production plant includes the crushing and grinding process, the screening process, the beneficiation process and so on. Today, we invite the expert from Shanghai MPM to take part in the interview. Welcome him to tell us iron ore processing production plant and the machines.

Expert: Good afternoon, everyone. Do you remember me? I’m your old friend. Nice to meet you again!

Host: Iron ore is one of the most familiar mineral. Our daily life can’t do without it. In our around, we can see its shadow.

Expert: Yes. Iron ore is the world's most common use metal. It is used in the various departments of national economy, and people's daily life. With the rapid and steady development of social economy, all walks of life have more and more demand for iron. Therefore, the use of iron ore resources has become a prominent problem in the development of our society. As the first step of iron ore resources utilization, crusher industry is becoming a growing industry.

Host: I think iron crushing and grinding process is the most common production plant. So can you introduce it for us?

Expert: I’m glad. The crushing and grinding process must can’t without crusher and grinder. We can use jaw crusher, impact crusher, cone crusher and so on. The grinder has trapezium mill, vertical mill and ultrafine mill. These machines are all suitable for iron ore processing. Mineral processing plant generally uses two or three stages crushing process to crush the iron ore. Jaw crusher is usual as the primary crushing machine. Impact crusher or cone crusher is usual as the second or third crushing machine. So is the grinding equipment. You can choose the one or two stages grinding process.

Host: For the iron ore, the beneficiation process is also important, isn’t it?

Expert: Yes. The beneficiation process is common for iron ore processing production plant. The beneficiation machine has the flotation machine, magnetic separation machine, spiral classifier, hydrocyclone, high-frequency screen and so on.

Host: Thank you for your wonderful introduction.

Expert: You are welcome.
