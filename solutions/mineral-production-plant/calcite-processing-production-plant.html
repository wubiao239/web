Host: Calcite is a calcium carbonate mineral. In the natural calcium carbonate, it is also the most common. For its processing methods, there are many different choices. The processing production plant is also diverse. So today, we will introduce the common production machine. Welcome the expert—Mr. Wang.

Mr. Wang: Good morning, everyone. I have studied it for many years. Today, I will try my best to do a better introduction.

Host: We know that we can use the calcite in many aspects. But there are also some people who don’t know our application. So can you first make a detailed introduction about calcite to help people to know it?

Mr. Wang: OK. It is a widespread mineral. Its application is very wide. In general, it is used as the raw materials in chemical industry, cement and other industrial. In metallurgical industry, it is used as the flux. In the construction industry, it is used in the production of cement and limestone. In the food industry, it can be used as the additives. It can also be used for plastic, paper and toothpaste. If you join the calcite in the glass production process, the glass will become translucent, especially suitable to be used as the glass lamp shade.

Host: According to what you said, calcite’s application is so wide. Then, its production methods must be diverse.

Mr. Wang: Yes. There are many methods and processing plant. The crushing machine is also diverse. It contains many machines, such as the crushers, grinder, vibrating screen, vibrating feeder, sand making machine and so on. For the crushing equipment, we usually use jaw crusher, cone crusher, impact crusher or hammer crusher. For the grinding equipment, we usually use vertical mill, trapezium mil, ultrafine mill, ball mill or Raymond mill. In addition, we also need vibrating feeder, vibrating screen, belt conveyor, elevators and so on. These crushers and grinders will be chose according to our production requirement.

Host: Thank you Mr. Wang. This is a great interview. Look forward to your next coming.

Mr. Wang: You are welcome. Hope to have the opportunity.
