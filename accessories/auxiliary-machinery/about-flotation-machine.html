Flotation machine is a common used machine. Every part works together and completes the flotation process. Today, we together acquaint its common parts and structural component. The following is the detailed information.

It has the pulp inlet, and strobe device adjusting the pulp surface. It is mainly composed of the groove welded by steel plate and the strobe welded steel plate and steel.

Stirring Device

It is used for mixing the slurry to prevent ore precipitation in the tank. Stirring device is mainly composed of a belt wheel, impeller, vertical shaft etc.. Impeller is made of wearable rubber.

The Inflatable Device

It consists of duct and the air inlet pipe. When the impeller rotates, the negative pressure is generated in the impeller cavity. Through the hollow pump suction, the air enters and disperses bubble formation group in the pulp. The pulp with a large number of bubbles by the rotating force of the impeller and is quickly thrown to the stator. So that the bubble further refine ore pulp, rotary motion and eliminate pulp flow in flotation cell. A large number of micro bubbles will do the vertical upward moving, and provide necessary conditions for flotation process.

The Mineralized Bubbles Exclusion Of Device

It is scraping the foam floating on the groove surface. The motor drives the gear reducer, and the reducer drives the scraper.

The Driving Mechanism

The motor drives the pulley on the shaft by the belt. The spindle is connected to both ends of the overall structure and the structure of the two. The big belt wheel uses the cone axis to connect with the drive shaft. The bearing seat is fixed on the beam casting, groove on the upper side of the bolts and nuts, a motor bracket is fixed on the upper beam body, a motor seat board is angle connecting structure, adjust the belt tensioning screw nut through the tightness. Steel plate net manufacturing belt, and has two handles.

The Tank

Each stirring mechanism has a groove. Tank is manufactured by steel plate. Below the groove, channel steel beams support the flotation machine. The groove can add the rubber lining according to user requirements.
