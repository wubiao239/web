Host: The common beneficiation equipment has the magnetic separation machine, flotation machine, spiral classifier, high frequency screen, hydrocyclone and so on. Among them, the magnetic separation machine is the important equipment. Today, let’s together discuss its wear parts and structural component. Help us understand the magnetic separation machine better. Welcome the expert—Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: Hello, everyone. I’m your old friend. I’m happy to see you again.

Host: Welcome your coming again, Mr. Wang! The magnetic separation machine is the most common dressing equipment. Can you first lead us to review it?

Mr. Wang: I’m glad. Magnetic separator is one of the most widely used and high versatility. It is suitable for the separation of magnetic substance with difference. Magnetic separation machine is widely used in mining, timber industry, ceramics, chemical, food industry etc.. For the mining, it is suitable for wet or dry magnetic separation of manganese ore, magnetite, pyrrhotite, roasted ore, ilmenite, hematite and limonite. It is also used for iron removing operation of coal, non-metallic minerals, building materials and other materials. With the development of our industry, its role will be larger and larger.

Host: The magnetic separation machine is so important. I think knowing its wear parts and structural component is also vital. So how about its main structure and parts.

Mr. Wang: Magnetic separator is mainly composed of a cylinder, magnetic system, tank body, and driving part. Cylinder uses the 2-3mm stainless steel plate roll welded tube. Cover is the aluminum parts, and connected with the stainless steel screw and barrel. Motor through reducer drives cylinder for rotary movement. Magnetic system is the open magnetic system, and installed in the cylinder. The work area of the groove is manufactured by stainless steel plate. The other parts of body of frame and groove body are used the ordinary steel welding. According to the different structure types of the tank body, magnetic separator is divided into concurrent flow, countercurrent, semi countercurrent.

Host: How wonderful! This is a detailed brilliant introduction. Thanks Mr. Wang’s coming. Look forward to your next coming!

Mr. Wang: You are welcome. I’m happy to take part in the interview. Hope to have the opportunity.
