Sand Washing Machine Common Accessories

Sand washing machine is the most important washing equipment. It is mainly composed of the feed hopper, vortex dynamic crushing cavity, the material screening device, impeller and the transmission shaft and the bearing etc.. The following is the detailed introduction on its common accessories and structure advantages.

The feed hopper is usually cylinder. It has good abrasion resistance, and is installed the wear ring to prevent no broken material to its strong friction. Hopper is an important transition device from the feeding device to the crusher.

The Vortex Dynamic Crushing Cavity

The impeller and the vortex dynamic crushing cavity is the main crushing part. It is the annular space is composed of two sections of cylinders. Through the impeller in the crusher internal high-speed rotation, it can form the material striking and friction.

The Material Screening Device

The material screening device is a material screening structure. It is mainly used as that when the materials have reached the standard, they will be sent from sand washing machine by the screening device. The other parts will again enter be washed under its action, and ultimately achieve the qualified material granularity.

The Impeller

The impeller is mainly made of a special material. It is installed in the two period of bearing. Through the highly rotating will enter into the impeller center material evenly split spread to all parts. Such a powerful shock caused by grinding materials.

The Bearing And The Transmission Device

The bearing is through the motor and the triangular belt to drive the impeller rotate. It mainly consists of the spindle, bearings and bearing seat.

The Lubrication System

The lubricating parts of sand washing machine in operation are the main bearing parts. It usually takes particular pump adding on bearing lubrication. The lubrication system is very important for the normal work and the service life.
