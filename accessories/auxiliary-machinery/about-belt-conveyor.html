Belt conveyer is mainly composed of the machine frame, belt, roller, a driving device, tension device, turnabout drum, braking device and cleaning device etc.. The following is the detailed introduction about belt conveyorâ€™s main accessories and structure.

The Belt

The belt is the main component of belt conveyer. It is not only the bearing member, but also the traction components. It is used for transporting materials and transporting traction. The belt can be divided into ordinary PVC belt, PU belt, antiskid belt food grade, logistics transport special belt, Teflon belt and high temperature resistant belt etc..

The Roller

Roller is used to support the belt and t moves with the belt. The role of roller is to support the belt, reducing the belt motion resistance, so that the belt sag does not exceed the prescribed limits. Ensure the smooth running of the belt. According to the use, the roller can be divided into the trough type roller, flat idler, buffer roller and self-aligning idler.

The Driving Device

The driving device is the source power of belt conveyor. Motor through the shaft coupling and reducer drives the transmission drum rotation. With the friction between the roller and the belt, make the belt conveyor run.

The Tension Device

Conveyor tensioning device has the role: ensure the belt with enough tension, to generate the needed frictional force between the drum and belt, and limit the belt at the supporting roller sag. Then, make the normal operation of belt conveyor. The common tension device has the spiral tensioning device and the weight dropper tensioning device.

The Turnabout Drum

Turnabout drum has the role of changing the winding direction of the belt, to form a closed ring. Turnabout drum can be used as a tail roller of conveyor. It can make the belt have different angle to change. Turnabout drum is made up of cast iron and steel plate. Because the rubber has elasticity, it can remove scraps on the drum. The turnabout drum has encapsulated and non-encapsulated.

The braking device Generally speaking, in order to prevent overload shutdown accident, it needs to set the brake device. It can reduce the accident rate.
