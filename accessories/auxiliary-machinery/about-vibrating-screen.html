Vibrating screen has several types. It includes the circular vibrating screen, linear vibrating screen and resonance screen. The two former are often referred to as the inertia vibrating screen. Their working principle and the structure are different. However, they have the same components, namely, vibrator, screen box, transmission device and vibration isolating device.

Circular vibrating screen is pure vibration vibrator, bearing eccentricity type and the belt wheel acentric type self-centering vibrator. Because the latter can make the vibration screen have the simplified structure, easy manufacture and long service life. So it has been widely used in practice. Linear vibrating screen has the box type and cylinder type vibrator. Resonance screen has the excitation system which is composed of nearly equal vibration quality.

The Screen Box

The sieve box is composed of the screen frame, screen surface and fastening device. The screen frame is composed of a side plate and beam. The board is made of steel plate. Beam is made up of steel pipe, steel or steel i-beam. They are connected by using the riveting, welding or high strength bolts and so on.

The Transmission Device

Vibration sieve generally uses the triangle belt transmission device. It has the simple structure. The vibrator speed can be arbitrary choice. But when in operation, the belt is easy to slip and cause clogging. Some use the coupling direct driving vibrating sieve. This can make the vibrator speed to maintain stability and prolong the service life. At present, there is also the gear transmission system with the high performance.

The Vibration Isolating Device

The vibration isolation devices are commonly used spiral spring, plate spring and the rubber spring. The spiral spring has the advantages of compact structure, small dimension. But the disadvantage is the small transverse stiffness. The sieve is prone to transverse vibration. The advantages of the plate spring are large lateral stiffness, which can eliminate the transverse vibration. The disadvantage is the large size, the installation is more difficult. The advantages of rubber spring are small in size, large rigidity, and good isolation performance. The disadvantage is that it doesnâ€™t adapt to large amplitude. Because the amplitude of vibration sieve is larger, the general use of vibration isolation device is the cylindrical helical spring.
