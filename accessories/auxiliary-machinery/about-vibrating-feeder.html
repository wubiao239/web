Host: Hello, everyone. Nice to meet you again. We know that the vibrating feeder is the important equipment. Then, do you know its main structure? Every part is vital for the machine. So today, we invite the expert—Mr. Wang to come here. He will give us a detailed analysis about the vibrating feeder common parts and structure. Welcome Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: Good morning, everyone! I’m happy to take part in the interview. Hope it will help you!

Host: The feeding equipment has many kinds of types. Why can the vibrating feeder occupy so big market?

Mr. Wang: With the market need, the feeding equipment will be more and more. But the vibrating feeder is developing. It has its unique advantages, such as simple structure, good performance, low noise, low energy consumption uniform and continuous feeding. The exciting force can be adjusted at any time. It meets the market demand. So it is popular.

Host: Vibrating feeder is the common equipment. Can you introduce its structure for us?

Mr. Wang: OK. I’m glad. Vibrating feeder is composed of a feeding trough, vibrator, spring, spring seat, a vibration motor and a transmission device etc.. Body vibration feeder vibration source is vibrator. The vibrator is composed of two eccentric shaft (active and passive) and gear pair. They work together to complete the normal operation of vibrating feeder.

Host: They cooperate with each other. So, how about its role in the working?

Mr. Wang: Vibrating feeder machine generates the centrifugal force by the rotation of the eccentric block of the vibrator. Then, it makes the screen box, vibrator to do the circular or approximately circular motion. Motor drives the driving shaft through the triangle belt. The driving shaft gear meshes with the driven shaft to rotate. The driven shaft and the driving shaft do the reverse rotation at the same time, so that the body vibration. Electric vibration feeder process is the use of electromagnetic vibrator driven feed tank along the inclined direction for periodic linear reciprocating motion to achieve.

Host: OK. Thank you, Mr. Wang. This is a wonderful interview.

Mr. Wang: You are welcome.
