Cone crusher is a promising crushing machine. With the development of the foundation construction, the application of cone crusher is more and more broad. At present, it is widely used in mining, smelting, building materials, highway, railway, water conservancy and chemical industry and many other departments. Cone crusher structure includes the frame, the eccentric sleeve, crushing cone, adjusting ring, lubricating system, hydraulic system, and insurance system. Today, letâ€™s together learn the cone crusher and know its common component and the wear parts.

The Frame Of Cone Crusher

Cone crusher frame is composed of the upper frame body and the lower frame body. Through the hydraulic cylinder, they are connected together. In the frame, there are the wear-resistant lining board and the protection cover. The upper frame body is the welding assembly. The top is welded with wear-resistant copper. The lower frame body is a whole cast steel.

The Eccentric Sleeve

Eccentric sleeve is installed the high lead bronze bushing. It adopts screw fixation. The lower part installs the large bevel gear and the thrust bearing by the bolt. Among them, the upper thrust bearing is the forged steel parts. The lower thrust bearing is the high lead bronze parts. In addition, the eccentric sleeve is also provided with a balance weight assembly, which uses the fixed bolt. The internal has the wearable protective lining board.

The Crushing Cone

The crushing cone of cone crusher is composed of upper and lower crushing wall broken wall. When the moving cone swings, near the discharge port of the crushing cavity, between the dynamic cone and the fixed cone, there is equal gap parallel strip, making the finished product granularity uniformity.

The Adjusting Ring

The adjusting ring is composed of a regulating ring, a clamping ring, cam follower and hydraulic motor. In the upper of the adjusting ring has the main frame fixing pin and a guide rod, to prevent the adjustment ring rotation in the main rack.

The Lubricating System

The lubrication system is composed of the lubricating oil tank, a motor oil pump, a filtering device, a cooling device and a control device.
