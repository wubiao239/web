Jaw Crusher Common Accessories

The accessories are the lifeblood for one machine. The jaw crusher is no exception. Every part has an important role for the normal work. Jaw crusher is the most common and most extensive crushing equipment. No matter what type of jaw crusher, its main component is basically same. Following is the common accessories and the performance introduction:

The flywheel of jaw crusher has two functions: balance groove wheel weight and store energy. The belt wheel also plays the role of the flywheel. Flywheel often is made up of the cast iron or cast steel. The minicomputer flywheel is often integral manufacturing. Flywheel installation should pay attention to the static balance.

The Movable Jaw

The movable jaw is supporting tooth plate parts. It is directly involved in the broken ore. So it is of sufficient strength and rigidity. Its structure should be sturdy and durable. The movable jaw is divided into box type and non-box. The movable jaw generally adopts casting structure.

The Toothed Plate

It is directly associated with the ore. Although its structure is simple, it has an effect on improving the productivity of jaw crusher, energy consumption, product size and crushing force. Tooth plate is subjected to a great impact, so its wear is very serious.

The Frame

The frame is composed of a front wall, back wall and side wall and the guide rail component. In the work, it will have great impact load, so it should have sufficient strength and rigidity. In general, the frame is made up of steel casting. For the small crusher, it also uses the cast iron to instead cast steel.

The Toggle Plate

The toggle plate is the most simple structure parts. But its role is very important. It is not only the moving jaw movement transmission parts, but also the insurance device of crusher. In the repeated impact extrusion, the toggle plate wear is serious. In order to improve the transmission efficiency, reduce wear, and prolong the service life, most toggle is the recoverable rolling type structure.
