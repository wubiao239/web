Sand making machine is also called the vertical shaft impact crusher (VSI). As we all know, it is mainly composed of the feed hopper, the material sieving device, vortex crushing cavity, impeller, bearing and the transmission shaft parts. The motor is the core component of the whole system sand machine. The transmission shaft and the bearing play the transmission function. The distributor is the material separation components. The impeller and the vortex crushing cavity are the main parts for crushing material. Today, let’s together learn its common accessories and wear parts.

The Feed Hopper

The feed hopper is usually cylindrical shape. It is an important transition device that the material from the feeding device to the crusher. In general, it is installed the wear ring to prevent no broken material strong friction to damage the device.

The Material Sieving Device

The material screening device is a material screening part. When the material is damaged, a part has reached the standard, and through the material screening device, is sent from sand making machine. The other part is not reached the standard, and does the second crushing in the crushing cavity, then ultimately achieve the qualified material granularity.

The Vortex Crushing Cavity

Vortex dynamic crushing cavity is the annular space, which is composed of two sections cylinders. It is through the impeller in the crusher internal high speed rotation to produce power for crushing material.

The Impeller

Impeller is made of a special material. It is mounted on the two ends of bearing. Through highly rotating, the material entering into the impeller center will spread to all parts. The powerful impact force will crush the material.

Bearing And Transmission Device

The bearing is made up of main spindle, bearings and bearing seat. It plays a driving role.

The Wear Parts

The wear parts of sand making machine have the upper (lower) wear plate, separatory cone, bulk plate, impact block, hammer, wear plate, impact plate, feed eye ring, and feed tube. These wear parts plays an important role for sand making machine’s normal working.
