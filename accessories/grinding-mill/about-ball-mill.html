Ball mill is a kind of important fine grinding equipment. It is suitable for grinding various ores, and is widely used in mineral processing, building materials and chemical industry etc.. According to the different production mode, specifications, unloading and the driving force ways, ball mill is divided into many types. But its main structure is roughly same. The following is the detailed information about the common wear accessories and the main composition structure.

Ball mill’s main structure has the body part and the transmission system. The former is composed of barrel body, lining board, partition board, the main bearings, and the material inlet and outlet device.

The Barrel Body

The cylinder of ball mill is one of the main working parts. It needs to have sufficient strength and rigidity. Strength of metal materials which requires cylinder manufacturing to high, the plastic is better, better mechanical properties and process performance. It can ensure the safe operation of the drum.

The Lining Board

  • Lining board of ball mill is used to protect the cylinder body from the grinding body and material and friction. Lining board is mostly made of metal material, and also has a small amount of non-metallic materials for manufacturing. Lining board in addition to protect the barrel body, influence also the movement rule of the grinding body. In order to adapt to the different working state, liner shape and materials are also different.

    • The Main Bearing

    The main bearing is the most commonly used sliding bearing. It is a key component of the ball mill. We must give full attention to lubrication problem of its. Generally use the centralized oil circulation lubrication. Also the way the minicomputer ring oil, oil ring lubrication or wool lubricant etc..

    The Transmission System

    The main transmission way of ball mill has the marginal and central transmission. In addition, there is no gear directly driven by the motor. Generally, most users choose the edge transmission, because the repair is convenient and the cost is low. With the large-scale of ball mill, it needs more and more power. So the transmission system is more important.
