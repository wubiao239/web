Host: Vertical mill is a promising grinding machine. Compared with other grinders, vertical mill has its unique advantages. And it is loved by most of customers. Do you understand the vertical mill’s main structure? Every part is essential for vertical mill. So today, we invite the grinder expert—Mr. Wang to take part in the interview. Welcome!

Mr. Wang: Good morning, everyone. This is my honored to come here. I will try my best to complete today’s introduction.

Host: As far as I know, the vertical mill is the important equipment. So can you first introduce it for us?

Mr. Wang: Of course. I’m glad. Vertical milling machine is based on the advanced technology at home and abroad, and combined with a variety of mill years students vertical milling machine production experience. It is an advanced grinding equipment design and development. Vertical mill sets the drying, crushing, grinding, and grading conveying in one. It is the ideal equipment for milling industry. At present, it can be widely used in cement, electric power, metallurgy, chemical, nonmetallic mineral industry. With the society development, its role will be larger and larger.

Host: So how about the vertical mill’s main structure and wear parts?

Mr. Wang: The main structure of vertical mill has the separator, grinding roll device, the grinding disc device, a pressure device, reducer, motor, shell and other components. They are indispensable for the normal work. The separator is an important component of milling products decision granularity. It consists of driving device, a speed adjustable guide vanes, rotor, casing, coarse powder blanking cone, outlet etc.. Separator is a powder selecting device with energy saving and fast and high efficient. The pressure device is to provide the milling pressure grinding roller parts. It consists of high pressure oil station, hydraulic cylinder rod, accumulator etc.. It can exert enough pressure to make the material crushing and grinding roller.

Host: We know that the grinding roll and grinding ring is the most vital crushing composition. They are also the wear parts.

Mr. Wang: Yes. The grinding roller is the main component of rolling grinding of materials. It is installed in the mill on the curved arm. Under the action of external force, it pressed disc materials. In the disk drive, turn milling roller, so that the material is compacted and crushed.

Host: Thank you, Mr. Wang!

Mr. Wang: You are welcome.
