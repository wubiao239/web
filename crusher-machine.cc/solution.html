Bauxite Production Line

The primary bauxite ore will be as large as 1.5m in diameter. First, the big material will fed into jaw crusher, by the time the ore after the first crushing will be less than 180mm. Then through the cone crusher also called secondary crushing, bauxite crushing plant equipment further reduces the size to less than 30mm. The crushed ore is fed into a stockpile, ready to be carried by conveyor belt to the refinery.
