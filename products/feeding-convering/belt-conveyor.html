Belt conveyor is a kind of mechanical friction drive with continuous way to transport material. In industrial production, the belt conveyor has been widely used, to ensure the continuity and automation production. It is widely used in metal and non-metallic mineral fields. Such as limestone, calcite, dolomite, barite, talc, calcium carbonate, gypsum, bentonite, glass etc. Its max feed size is 400-1200mm. Its production capacity is 40-480T/H.

Main Structure

Belt conveyer is mainly composed of a machine frame, a conveyer belt, a belt roller, tensioning device, transmission device, etc. The machine body uses the high quality steel plate to connect. The machine frame is provided with a roller, roller, and used for driving and supporting conveyor belt. There are two ways of the deceleration motor drive and electric roller drive.

Feature and Advantages

  1. Large conveying capacity, high adaptability: Different type machines can meet customer requirements of different conveying capacity; belt conveyor can be used in the environmental temperature of -20℃to +40 ℃, and the conveying material temperature is below 50 ℃.
  2. Component standardization, convenient repair: The production is standardization, and the machine has the advantages of simple structure, rapidly repair operation.
  3. Non slip handle, strong stability: Aiming at the possible slip between material and belt machine, our company provides a human- shaped belt machine, solving this problem effectively, enhancing the stability of the work of belt conveyor.
  4. Model diverse, flexible use: According to the requirements of the different transportation technology, it can be single use, also can use multiple combinations, or with other equipment to form a horizontal or inclined conveying system.
