BWZ Heavy Duty Apron Feeder

BWZ heavy duty apron feeders are based on the modern advanced technology and application extensively, to independently research and develop the efficient delivery device. It is suitable for short distance and uniform delivery. BWZ heavy duty apron feeder is applied in metal mining, engineering construction, cement industry and coal industry. And it is suitable for ferrous metal ore, non-ferrous metal ore, cobble, limestone, dolomite, granite, diabase, basalt, rhyolite, coal etc. Its feed size is 0-1600mm. Its production capacity is 150-1000mm.

Main Structure

BWZ heavy duty apron feeder mainly consists of driving device, the spindle device, belt device, supporting device, tension device, frame, hopper, and so on. Among them, the driving device comprises motor, reducer, coupling, torque rod. The main shaft device is composed of driving shaft, drive sprocket, bearing seat etc. Chain device is composed of traction chain track, trough board etc. Supporting device is composed of a plurality of rows of rollers. Tensioning device is composed of tail shaft, changing direction wheel, sliding bearing seat, pull rod, composite spring etc.

Feature and Advantages

  1. The device uses the standard tank chain transmission, and has the advantages of high strength, large force, no need of lubrication, the small error, long life without repair.
  2. The carrier plate is made of welded alloy steel, and has the high strength, long service time.
  3. Use the vertical cross reducer, and floating suspension installation, convenient maintenance.
  4. Between the main shaft and the chain wheel is adopted the expansion sleeve keyless connection. It is not only good strength, but also simple and convenient assembly and disassembly.
  5. Using variable frequency motor drive, is easy to operate and control, and uniform feeding accuracy.
  6. Buffer design of tensioning device, reduces equipment vibration and impact.
  7. Particle size is big, and can bear larger cabin pressure.
  8. It has a strong environmental adaptability, that is to say it can run in the severe working conditions.
