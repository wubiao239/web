Vibrating feeder

  • Your location:

In the crushing production line, vibration feeder can send materials to crusher evenly and continuously, at the same time, it also can screen materials roughly. It is widely used in metallurgy, coal, mineral processing, artificial sand, recycling of construction waste, building materials, chemical industry, and abrasive industries. And it is suitable for cobble, granite, basalt, quartz, iron ore, limestone, etc. Its max feed size is 300-800mm. Its production capacity is up to 30-1000T/H.

Main Structure

Vibrating feeder consists of the vibrating frame, spring, vibrator, motor vibrating device and motor. The vibrator is composed of two fixed eccentric shaft and gear pair. Spring is made of spring steel. It is not easy to damage, and has long service life.

Working Principle

The vibration source of body vibration feeder is the vibration exciter. The vibrator is composed of two eccentric shaft and gear pair. Motor drives the driving shaft through the triangle belt. Drive shaft gear meshed the driven shaft to make it rotate. The driving shaft and the driven shaft reverse rotation at the same time, so that the body vibrates, and the material continuously flows, achieving the purpose of conveying material.

Feature and Advantage

  1. Have the advantages of simple structure, stable vibration, uniform feeding, continuous performance is good, the exciting force can be adjusted;
  2. At any time to change and control the flow, convenient operation;
  3. Eccentric block as the excitation source, low noise, low power consumption, good regulation performance, without the phenomenon of red material;
  4. Using a closed body can prevent dust pollution;
  5. The installation and adjustment are convenient, and the weigh and volume are small. The maintenance is convenient.
