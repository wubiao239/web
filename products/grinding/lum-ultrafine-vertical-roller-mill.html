LUM Ultrafine Vertical Roller Mill

  • Your location:
  • Home
  • Products
  • Grinding equipments

LUM ultrafine vertical roller mill is set ultrafine, grading, and conveying with a suit. It is widely used in chemical industry, metallurgy, non-metallic mineral industries. And it is suitable for grinding the materials that Mohs hardness is below level 5, density is below 3.2, and the compressive strength is below 150Mpa, Such as limestone, calcite, barite, talc, dolomite and other nonmetal ore. The feed size is better less than 10mm. The production is 5-18T/H.

Main Structure

LUM ultrafine vertical roller mill mainly includes the host, reducer, analyzer, fan, power collector, electric control cabinet and so on. The auxiliary consists of the jaw crusher, bucket elevator, electromagnetic vibrating feeder, and storage bin.

Feature and Advantages

  1. The automatic control system can realize remote operation, and the operation is simple.
  2. The grinding roller and the grinding disc can’t contact, and the grinding roller and the lining board are made of high quality material, to less abrasion and long service life.
  3. Maintenance is quick and convenient. The operation can realize in the outside of the machine body, reducing downtime.
  4. The quality of products is high, and in the same fineness grade condition, it can save 30%~50% of energy, and has good economic benefit.
  5. The system is working in confined pressure conditions. It can reduce dust to escape, reduce noise, clean environment, and full compliance with the relevant provisions of the state.
  6. The main machine adopts frequency control. It is more energy saving than electromagnetic adjustable speed, and high crushing efficiency.
  7. The design of roller sleeve and liner grinding curve is easier to form a material layer than the ordinary grinder, improving work efficiency.
  8. The grinding roller lubrication is using separate lubrication station. It can make the grinding roller bearing to obtain the full lubrication, and can avoid the oil storing too much and leakage phenomenon.
