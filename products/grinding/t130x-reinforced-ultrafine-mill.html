T130X Reinforced Ultrafine Mill

  • Your location:
  • Home
  • Products
  • Grinding equipments

Based on the MTM trapezium mill, T130X reinforced ultrafine mill passes through inheritance and optimization, and forms its own unique characteristics. T130X reinforced ultrafine mill is mainly used in metallurgy, building materials, chemical, mineral and other industries. And it is suitable for feldspar, calcite, talc, barite, marble, rare earth, slag, clay, bentonite, gypsum, graphite. The maximum feed particle size should be less than 30mm. The finished product size is between 0.074~0.038mm. The production capacity is 1.8~6.5T/H.

Working Principle

After by jaw crusher preliminary broken, the material enters into the hopper by elevator. Through the electromagnetic vibrating feeder, the material is taken into the host evenly and quantitatively. After grinding, the material is blown in the powder selecting machine by fan airflow and does the classification work. The material that does not meet the size requirement, falls into the host to grinding again. The material that meets the size requirement, enters into the cyclone powder collector. The materials do separation and collection. The finished product powder is discharged from the discharging device. The whole system is a negative pressure closed cycle, to ensure the production site clean and environmental protection.

Feature

  1. Using bypass powder collector, with dust isolation room, effectively solve the problem of dust escaping. Compared with general powder collector, its collecting efficiency is higher, and can collect the particles that the general powder collector is different to collect.
  2. Between the body and the base is completely soft connection, without rigid contact.
  3. The powder selector machine adopts frequency control. It is more energy saving than the electromagnetic speed regulation, and the degree of automation is higher.
  4. High density selected powder blade can improve the fineness and yield of selected powder, and is conducive to improve the quality of finished products.
  5. The host and the reducer are used pin coupling with elastic sleeve, improving equipment’s reliability.
  6. The speed reducer and the motor adopt the triangle belt to transmit power, and it is conducive to the overload protection.
  7. The base is made up of nodular cast iron with high strength and anti-vibration crack. It has good endurance.
