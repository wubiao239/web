Ball Mill

The ball mill is the key equipment for milling, especially suitable for after first being crushed material. It is widely used in cement, silicate products, new building materials, refractory materials, chemical fertilizer, black non-ferrous metal processing, glass ceramics and other production industry. It is suitable for grinding various ores and other materials. According to the machining process, ball mill can be divided into two kinds grinding method: dry-type grind and wet-type grinding.

Main Structure

Ball mill is mainly composed of a feeding part, discharging part, a rotary part, a transmission part and other main parts. The transmission part comprises a speed reducer, small gear, electric motor, electronic control etc. Hollow shaft adopts steel castings. The liner is detachable. The slewing big gear adopts gear hobbing with casting. The cylinder body inlays the wear-resistant liner, with good resistance.

The ball mill host comprises a cylinder body, cylinder liner, bearings, and drive part, such as a motor, and a transmission gear, pulley, triangle belt etc.

Ball mill fittings have a big gear, a small gear, a hollow shaft, gear ring, a big gear ring, a steel ball, diaphragms, transmission device, bearing, and end lining etc.

Feature and Advantages

  1. The spindle bearing is improved, replacing the original sliding bearing. This will reduce friction, and energy consumption. It is easy to start the mill.
  2. Retain the ordinary grinder’s end cap structure, with large caliber inlet and outlet, and large processing quantity.
  3. The structure of feeder is simple and split installation.
  4. The operation of equipment is smooth, and reduces the downtime parking repair time, improving the production efficiency.
  5. The capacity and fineness can be adjusted by adjusting the diameter of the ball.
  6. The shape of final product is uniform, and most of them are round.
