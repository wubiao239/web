LM Vertical Roller Mill

  • Your location:

LM vertical roller mill is developed by using the advanced technology at home and abroad. It sets crushing, drying, grinding, classification and conveying with a suit, and is the ideal grinding product. It is widely used in cement, metallurgy, chemical industry, hydropower, mineral and other industries. And it is suitable for grinding of cement, coal, feldspar, calcite, fluorite, talc, iron ore, etc. The maximum feed particle size is 38~55mm. And the production capacity is 10~50T/H.

Main Structure

LM Vertical Roller Mill mainly consists of the classifier, the transmission arm, grinding plate, grinding roller, hydraulic device, pressure device, reducer, feed opening, limiting device, and so on. The grinding roller is the main part to grind the material. The pressure device brings press for the grinding roller to grind material.

Application of LM series vertical roller mill

LM series vertical roller mill is used to solve the problem of low yield, high energy consumption of the industrial mill technical problems, absorb the advanced technology of Europe and combining our years of advanced milling machine Design and manufacturing concept and demand of the market, after years of painstaking the improved design of large grinding equipment. Vertical mill adopts the structure design is reasonable and reliable, with advanced technology, drying, grinding, Powder selection, promotion in one, especially in the large grinding process, fully meet customer demand, the main technical, economic indicators have reached the international advanced level.

Feature and Advantages

  1. LM vertical roller mill is the only mill with the drying system of hot blast stove. And its drying ability is good, solving the problem of the high humidity materials, saving energy.
  2. By adjusting the temperature of hot air can meet the requirements of different humidity material grinding.
  3. The automatic control system can realize remote control. The operation is simple, and reliable.
  4. Sealing pressure system makes that there is no dust, clean and environmental protection.
  5. The equipment’s vibration is small, and its noise is low.
  6. Set crushing, drying, grinding, grading and conveying into a whole. The layout is compact, covering an area of only 50% for ball mill system.
  7. The grinding roller and the lining board are made of high quality material, and the service life is long.
  8. The device can open layout, saving the investment cost.
  9. The residence time of material in the mill is short. It is easy to detect and control the product size and chemical composition, reducing duplication of milling, making product quality more stable.
