Raymond mill is a grinding mill with a long history, and it is the ancestor of the trapezium mill. It is mainly used for small and medium-sized mines, chemical, building materials, metallurgy, refractory materials, pharmaceutical, cement and other industries. And it is suitable for gypsum, talc, calcite, limestone, marble, dolomite, barite, granite, kaolin, bauxite, iron oxide, iron ore etc. The max feeding grain size is 20-30mm. The grain size of product is 1.6-0.045mm. The production capacity is 1.2-11T/H.

Main Structure of Raymond Mill

Raymond mill is mainly composed of host, blower, analysis, separator, piping device, motor and other components. The host is composed of frame, wind inlet volute, blade, grinding roll, grinding ring, cover casing and motor. Auxiliary equipment has jaw crusher, bucket elevator, electromagnetic vibration feeder, electric control cabinet. According to the scene, the user can choose flexibly.

Working Principle

The material enters into the host from the side inlet hopper. Rely on the grinding roll device suspend in the host plum flower shelf, and grind roller revolves around the vertical axis, and itself rotation. The material is thrown into the space between the grinding roller and grinding ring. Under the action of grinding roller, the material is crushed and grinded.
