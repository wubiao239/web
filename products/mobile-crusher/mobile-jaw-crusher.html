Mobile Jaw Crusher

Mobile jaw crusher station is one of the most new rock crushing equipment. It is mainly used in the materials processing which often requires the removal operation, such as metallurgy, hydropower, chemical industry, and building materials, especially for the liquidity stone operations of highway, railway, hydropower engineering and other operations. In the production process, according to the processing raw material’s type, size and product materials requirements, users can use a variety of configuration.

Structure Configuration

Mobile jaw crusher mainly consists of vibrating feeder, jaw crusher, vibrating screen, belt conveyor, and so on. In the actual production, according to the specific requirements, the user can assemble different crushing equipment. If you need grind mill, you can also add a grinding equipment.

After selecting the equipment, you can also according to the requirements of different crushing process, to select the "first crushing then sieving" or "first sieving then crushing" sequence to assemble. You can choose the two section crushing and screening system of coarse crushing and fine crushing, or the three section crushing and screening system of coarse crushing, medium crushing and fine crushing.

The whole structure configuration of the mobile jaw crusher is according to the specific requirements of the crushing, screening and grinding.

Feature and Advantages

  1. Integrated unit installation form eliminates the complicated installation of split components, reducing the material and working hour consumption.
  2. The reasonable and compact space layout enhances the flexibility of site stationed.
  3. The operating line is short. The different crushing equipment were installed in the independent mobile chassis, and mobile is very convenient.
  4. Reduce the material transportation cost. It can process the material in the scene, and need not be removed from the scene.
  5. Strong adaptability of flexible configuration, the wheelbase is short, and the turning radius is small, so it can work in ordinary highway and in the region to flexibly drive.
  6. It has the reliable performance and convenient repair.
