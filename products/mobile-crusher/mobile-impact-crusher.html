Mobile impact crusher is the mobile crushing equipment, which is the impact crusher as the host. In the crushing link, impact crusher is general as the secondary crusher and is behind the jaw crusher. It is mainly used in the metallurgy, hydropower, chemical industry, and building materials, especially for highway, railway, hydropower engineering and other operations. In the production process, according to the processing raw material’s type, size and product materials requirements, users can use a variety of configuration.

Application

It is widely used in mining, coal, waste and construction waste recycling, earthwork, city infrastructure, road or construction sites and other field operations.

The treatment of the surface soil and a variety of other materials; separation of viscous coagulation aggregate; construction and demolition industry; the sieving after crushing; quarrying industry.

The application is in the cobble, rocks (granite, limestone, andesite, diabase, basalt etc.), ore tailings, stone chip of artificial sand.

Feature and Advantages

  1. Strong adaptability of flexible configuration, it can work in ordinary highway and in the region to flexibly drive;
  2. It is easy to adjust the crushing gap, enable to control grain-size and keep good particle shape.
  3. Vehicle traction, compact structure, stable and strong;
  4. A variety of models and other models can be fast combination, to meet the different needs of production and operation.
  5. It has the reliable performance and convenient maintenance;
  6. The mobile impact crusher uses our company’s excellent performance of crusher, vibrating screen and feeder, and quality is stable and reliable.
