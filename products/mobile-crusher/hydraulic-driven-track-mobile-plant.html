Hydraulic-Driven Track Mobile Plant

  • Your location:
  • Home

  • Mobile Crushing Plant

Hydraulic-driven track mobile plant is our company’s independent research and development mobile crushing plant in order to meet the market demand. The crushers are widely used in mining ore crushing, recycling of construction waste, construction aggregate production, highway, railway, road and bridge construction and other industries. It is suitable for crushing of granite, basalt, diabase, shale, sandstone, gravel, limestone, iron ore, copper ore, manganese ore, coal gangue, and non-ferrous metal ore. The feed size is 1-750mm. The production capacity is 90-450T/H.

Main Structure

All parts of hydraulic-driven track mobile plant are completely airborne, and mainly includes the strong frame, crushing machine, feeder, vibrating screen, conveyor belt, a motor and a control box.

Working Principle

The material is transported evenly to the crusher by the feeder. After primary crushing, through circular vibrating screen, the system forms a closed system, realizing the circulation crushing of the material. The finish product is output by belt conveyor. Hydraulic-driven track mobile plant can remove the circular vibrating screen according to the actual production need, realizing the material directly preliminary broken. The use of mobile devices supporting, makes flexible and convenient operation.

Feature and Advantage

  1. Use our company’s excellent performance of the crusher, vibrating screen and feeder, and its quality is stable and reliable.
  2. Operating system selects the imported servo pilot operating system, with light manipulation and accurate control, and can realize stepless speed walking.
  3. Chassis adopts caterpillar fully rigid hull structure, and has the high strength, good passing ability, and good adaptability to upland and wetland.
  4. The engine can add protection low temperature preheating starting device. The device can be easily started in low temperature environment of -25 C°.
  5. According to different requirements, the mobile crushing station can’t only single group independent operation, also can be used in combination, or according to the scene combined into other system.
  6. Equipped with Cummins diesel engine, and it has the advantages of excellent performance, low fuel consumption, low noise, and excellent performance.
