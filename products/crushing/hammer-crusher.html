Hammer crusher is using the high speed rolling hammer body to collide with material. It can be used for two situation of dry and wet crushing. Hammer crusher is widely used in mining, metallurgy, building materials, thermal power, refractories, quarrying and other industrial parts. It is suitable for medium crushing and fine crushing, such as limestone, aluminum ore, coke, oil shale, media, chalk, gypsum and other material.

Main Structure

Hammer crusher is mainly composed of the main case, the rotor, liner and combat board etc. The main case includes the lower shell body, the upper cover, left and right walls. Each part is connected into a whole through the bolt. The rotor is the main working part of hammer crusher. The rotor is composed of spindle, hammer disc, a pin shaft, a hammer head etc.

Working Principle

Under the action of the electric motor, the rotor rotates at high speed. The material evenly enters into the crushing chamber. Then material is broken by the high speed rotary hammer head. At the same time, under the action of itself gravity, material rushes from the high-speed rotating hammer head towards the baffle plate and grate bar. Then the material that their size is larger than the sieve pore size, continue to be crushed and grinded by hammer. The suitable materials are discharged from the sieve plate.

Feature

  1. Hammer head adopts the new casting technology, and has the advantages of wear resistance, impact resistance.
  2. The sealing structure solves the problems of leakage of ash and dust pollution in crushing workshop.
  3. The hammer crusher is compact structure, beautiful appearance, good wear resistance, and easy maintenance.
  4. The feed size is big, crushing ratio is high, and the product particle size of is average.
  5. It is the light weight, and high efficiency.
  6. The overall design of beautiful shape, compact structure, less wearing parts, convenient repair, is the upgrading of products.
