PE Jaw Crusher

PE jaw crusher is the most commonly used crushing equipment. It is widely used in metallurgy, chemical industry, construction, water conservancy and hydropower, highway, railway and other industries. PE jaw crusher is usually used for primary crushing. The highest compressive strength of broken material is 320 Mpa. It is suitable for crushing granite, marble, basalt, limestone, quartz stone, gravel, iron ore, copper ore and so on. The feed size is 425-1020mm, the production capacity is 45-800T/H.

Structure

PE jaw crusher basically has the following several parts: jaw stock, transmission gear, eccentric shaft, moveable jaw crusher, side lining, fixed jaw plate, set adjustment, safety device, flywheel, grooved wheel, toggle plate, and so on. Its wear parts mainly have the spring, spring tension rod, side plate, fixed jaw crusher, moveable jaw plate, toggle plate, bearing, and gasket.

Working Principle

The motor drives the eccentric shaft to rotate through a belt and a belt pulley. The movable jaw plate does the periodic reciprocating motion around the eccentric shaft. Sometimes, the movable jaw plate near to the fixed jaw plate, sometimes it leaves fixed jaw plate. When the movable jaw plate close to the fixed jaw plate, the ore, between the two jaw plate, is positioned by extrusion, bending, fracture and broken; when the movable jaw plate away from the fixed jaw plate, under the action of gravity, the broken ore is discharged from the discharge port.

Features

  1. Advanced machine tools, using CNC numerical control machine tool, and high machining accuracy;
  2. It uses the advanced painting technology. Do the high-pressure sandblasting in sandblasting room, then paint. The paint is tightly combined with iron, so the material is not easy to rust;
  3. The main components of the eccentric shaft, bearing, jaw board are using high quality raw material;
  4. The crushing chamber is deep and without dead zone, increasing the capacity and the output;
  5. Using Siemens motor, advanced technology, high quality;
  6. Have the advantages of simple structure and convenient repair;
  7. Stable performance, and low operation cost;
  8. Uniform product size, and big crushing ratio.
