PF Impact Crusher

PF impact crusher is to crushing material by impact. It is used in metallurgy, mine, cement, chemical, refractories, ceramics, road construction, water conservancy and hydropower industry. PF jaw crusher is suitable for crushing materials that length does not exceed 500mm, and the compressive strength is not more than 350MPa, such as: barite, calcite, talc, rare earth, kaolin, coke, gypsum. The maximum feed particle size is250~500mm, and production capacity is 50~260T/H.

Structure

PF series jaw crusher mainly has the following several parts: rotor assembly, impact plate, machine body part, adjustment device, transmission part, and so on. Specifically, it is mainly composed of rotor, impact plate, impact rack, impact plate, hammer, side liner plate, feeder opening, chassis, rear case. The wear parts have hammer, impact plate and side liner plate, and so on.

Working Principle

The rotor does the high-speed rotation under the driving of a motor. The material impacts with the hammer on the rotor. Then, under the hitting of hammer, the material is broken. The crushed material is impacted to the impact plate and broken again. Finally, the material is discharged from the discharge port. Adjust the clearance between the rotor frame and the impact frame, and it can be achieved the purpose of changing the material discharging size and material shape.

Feature

  1. It has big crushing ratio, and higher crushing efficiency;
  2. Have the advantages of simple structure and low price;
  3. The particle size is adjustable and convenient. The upper part is provided with a regulating device, and it can change the particle size by adjusting the space of the rotor and impact lining board;
  4. Particle is a cube, no tension crack, and the grain shape is good;
  5. Repair convenient and simple and reliable operation: The machine comes with open top device, and can carry out internal inspection and repair by opening the frame;
  6. Unique structure and high efficiency and energy saving: The machine uses the non key link, high chromium plate hammer, unique impact plate, abrasion resistance, high production efficiency.
  7. The device has the advantages of small volume, light weight, wide range of application.
