PFW Impact Crusher

PFW impact crusher is my company’s latest product, which is based on PF impact crusher and my company’s 20 years experiences. It is mainly used in metallurgy, mining, cement, chemical, refractory materials, ceramic industries, highway construction, water conservancy project and so on. It is suitable for various kinds of soft and hard ore for coarse crushing, finely homework, such as limestone, feldspar, calcite, talc, barite, clay, kaolin, gypsum, graphite, etc. The feed size is 250-700mm. The production capacity is 70-400T/H.

Main Structure

PFW series jaw crusher is mainly made up of the rotor part, impact support, frame, screw clamshell device (or hydraulic cover open devices) and transmission parts and other components. The wear parts mainly have the rotor, impact plate, hammer and so on. PFW impact crusher has two kinds of cavity type: the two-crusher and the three-crusher. The materials are crushed between the rotor and the impact plate. Through the hydraulic cover open devices, the maintenance personnel can maintain the internal of impact crusher. PFW impact crusher has the outstanding performance advantage, good discharging materials grain sharp and wider scope of application, making users to effectively reduce production and construction cost, operation cost.

Feature

  1. Word-class manufacturing process and the most significant end material;
  2. PFW impact crusher increases the hydraulic jacking device, and convenient replacement of wearing parts;
  3. Selection of hydraulic control system, by means of fast adjusting the outlet size and granularity;
  4. The design of heavy rotor, and strict detection means, ensure the high quality rotor, and make the special heavy rotor access to the higher moment of inertia；
  5. The crushing efficiency of crescent hammer is higher than the straight line type;
  6. Plate hammer fixing device is unique, and has the higher reliability；
  7. Bearing seat with integral cast steel structure, ensures the machine running stability;
  8. Remove the square steel components on the frame, simplifying the design structure;
  9. Selection of more large specification bearing, so it has the higher load capability;
  10. There are two kinds of cavity: the two chamber type and three chamber type, to meet the requirements of the vast majority of coarse crushing, medium, fine work.
