PEW Jaw Crusher

PEW series jaw crusher is a new type crusher and the most popular product, which is based on the development and research of PE jaw crusher. PEW series jaw crusher is widely used in construction waste crushing, ore crushing, water conservancy and hydropower, highway, railway, bridge construction, construction aggregate production industry. It is suitable for granite, marble, basalt, limestone, iron ore, copper ore etc. Its feed size is 150-930mm, and the production capacity is 12-650T/H.

Main Structure

PEW jaw crusher adopts the most advanced crushing technology and manufacturing process. PEW series of jaw crusher is mainly consisted of frame, the rotation part of the eccentric bearing, the working part of the crushing cavity, the safety unite, the adjustment devices of the discharging opening and so on. Specifically, it mainly includes: the movable jaw crusher, frame, fixed jaw plate, movable jaw crusher, movable jaw guard plate, pulley, eccentric shaft, toggle plate, spring rod and hydraulic adjustment system.

Feature

  1. Adopts the world class manufacturing process and the most high-end manufacturing materials;
  2. The movable jaw assembly is made up of high quality steel castings. The heavy eccentric shaft is processing with forging billet. This makes the machine has superior reliability, and more durable;
  3. Bearing seat adopts the integral cast steel structure, to ensure fully cooperate with broken frame, greatly enhancing the radial strength of the bearing seat;
  4. Using the finite element analysis technology makes the machine have the higher the intensity;
  5. The crushing chamber adopts symmetrical "V" shape structure, to make that the actual inlet width is consistent with the nominal inlet width;
  6. With the wedge block discharge port adjusting device. Compared with the old fashioned shim adjustment, it is much simpler, safer and faster;
  7. The selection of the eccentric shaft bearing is more durable. The higher load capacity and effective labyrinth seal make that the bearing service life is greatly prolonged;
  8. The latest the tooth protecting plate, increases the effective length of jaw plate, improve the yield;
  9. Use centralized lubrication device, and make the bearing lubrication more convenient;
  10. Mounted the heavy guard board on the bearing seat, avoids the accidental damage of bearing seat.
