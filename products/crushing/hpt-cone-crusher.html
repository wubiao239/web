HPT multi-cylinder hydraulic cone crusher is a new generation of high efficiency crusher. Based on the breakthrough optimization of equipment structure, HPT cone crusher significantly enhances the operational performance and crushing capacity. It is mainly used in building stone crushing and metal ore crushing and other fields. And it is suitable for cobble, limestone, dolomite, granite, diabase, basalt, rhyolite, ferrous metal ore etc. The feed size is 10-350mm. And the production capacity is 50-1200T/H.

Introduction

HPT hydraulic cone breaker is based on the introduction of the international latest fragmentation technology, and researches and develops the world's advanced level high efficiency cone crusher. Compared with other cone crusher, the crushing efficiency of HPT multi-cylinder hydraulic cone crusher improves by 5% to 15%, greatly accelerating the crushing process, reducing the cost. The new HPT hydraulic cone crusher adopts the “U” type crushing cavity, and the output increases by 35%. The application scope is more widely. HPT cone crusher has become the new generation crushing machine for the building crushing industry and mining crushing industry.

Feature and Advantage

  1. Operation control is more intelligent and humanized.
  2. The integrated hydraulic lubrication system and centralized automatic intelligent control system save the investment cost and real-time monitoring the running state of equipment.
  3. Further optimization of control system is more stable and reliable, guaranteeing the safe and stable operation of equipment.
  4. From the insurance clear cavity to adjusting and locking, all processes use the full hydraulic operation, making it more stable and reliable work, convenient operation.
  5. Product shape is cubic. The fine grain content is high, increasing product quality, and reducing the production cost of equipment and the whole system.
  6. Equipped with multi medium crushing and fine crushing cavity type, and it is better to meet medium crushing and fine crushing process requirements, to achieve a multi-purpose.
