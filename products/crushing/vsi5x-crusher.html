VSI5X(sand making machine) is a new high efficiency sand making machine. It is based on VSI sand making machine, and independently research and develop a new generation products. VSI5X sets three broken mode in one, and has become the core equipment of sand industry mechanism. It is suitable for crushing cobble, quartz, limestone, coal gangue, basalt, granite, and diabase. VSI5X is used for making artificial sand. Its feed size is 30-50mm. Its production capacity is 70-640T/H.

Main Structure of Sand Making Machine

Sand making machine is made up of the host parts and the rotor parts. The host parts includes feed hopper, feed hopper lifting device, the center of rotor, shaft assembly, a motor drive device, stent, thin oil lubrication station (external link). And the rotor parts includes impact block, upper and lower flow channel plate, hammer, swing round hole protecting ring, wear plate, anti-rotation barrier strip, impeller welding.

The wear parts mainly have the upper shaft sleeve, the lower shaft sleeve, a damping rubber, swinging wheel supporting export ring, upper / lower flow channel plate, the impact block, sub feed cone, wear plates, the thrower head, blanking ring lining, feeding tube, upper / down guard board, hopper ring lining, distributing disc, bulk disk, peripheral supporting plate, cylindrical roller bearings, deep groove ball bearings, thrust ball bearing, expansion sleeve, the conveyor belt, oil seal, O ring seal.
