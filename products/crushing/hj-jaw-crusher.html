HJ Jaw Crusher

HJ jaw crusher is based on the foundation of introducing, absorbing domestic and foreign advanced technology. According to customer demand, our company tries to research the modern high performance crusher. HJ jaw crusher is widely used in the construction stone crushing, metal ore crushing and solid waste treatment. It is suitable for cobble, limestone, dolomite, granite, diabase, basalt, ferrous metal ore, non-ferrous metal ore, construction waste, coal gangue, tailings. The feed size is 150-800mm. the production capacity is 110-650T/H.

Working principle

The crushing way of HJ jaw crusher is curved extrusion. Motor drives host slot wheel through a triangle belt, and then drives the eccentric shaft and moving jaw to rotate. When movable jaw drives the movable jaw plate to the fixed jaw plate movement, the material is crushed or split pieces. When the movable jaw body drives the movable jaw plate away from the fixed jaw plate, the broken material is discharged from the discharge port in the lower part of the jaw plate. With the continuous rotation of the motor, the crusher periodically crushes and discharges.

Feature

  1. Under the condition of same power, the output is greatly improved, and the performance is also greatly improved.
  2. Through the optimization of the whole structure and the weight part, HJ jaw crusher has been greatly improved in vibration. Compare with the same specifications crushers, it has higher stability.
  3. This machine adopts E type frame. The movable jaw part adopts the heat-treated forged eccentric shaft, with the labyrinth seal design and large bearing, so that the device is firm and durable, more reliable operation.
  4. The maintenance is more convenient, and the operation is simpler.
  5. Covers area is smaller, so that it can play a greater role in the limited space, and is applicable to various crushing process.
