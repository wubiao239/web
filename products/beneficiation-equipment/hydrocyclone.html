Hydrocyclone

The grinding and classification is a very important link in mineral separation production. As the important classification equipment, hydrocyclone has a very wide range of application. Hydraulic cyclone is used to separate the heavier coarse sediments and removal of other material in the sewage, sometimes it is used for sludge dewatering. At present, the extension devices of foreign metal mining are using the hydrocyclone.

Structure

Hydrocyclone are mainly composed of the working barrel body, the ore feeding pipe, the overflow pipe, overflow catheter and sand export etc. Among them, the working barrel body is composed of a hollow cylinder in the upper portion, and the inverted cone in the bottom part communicated with a cylinder.

Application

Hydrocyclone is mainly used for grading, sorting, thickening and desliming in the mineral processing industry. When the hydrocyclone is used as the classification equipment, it is mainly used to as the grinding classification system with grinding mill. As the desliming equipment, it can be used for gravity separation plant desliming. When it is used as thickening and dewatering device, it can be used to dressing tailings thickening evacuation to backfill in underground mining tunnel.

Feature and Advantages

  1. There is no moving part of hydrocyclone, and the structure is simple;
  2. The production capacity of unit volume is large, and the area occupation is small;
  3. High classification efficiency is up to 80% ~ 90%, and the classification grain size is fine;
  4. The cost is low, and material consumption is little;
  5. The disassembly and maintain are easy.
