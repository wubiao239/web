Magnetic separation machine consists of wet magnetic separator and dry magnetic separation machine. It is one of the most widely used and high universality in the aircraft industry. And it is suitable for the separation of the difference magnetic substance. Magnetic separation machine is mainly to remove the iron powder from the powder. It is widely used in the recycling of resources, wood industry, mining industry, ceramics, chemical, food and other plant.

Structure and Working Principle

Electromagnetic high gradient magnetic separator placed the separation tank in pin mounted within the solenoid coil, which is equipped with a certain number of magnetic conductive stainless steel wool (or steel plate mesh). After the coil excitation, the magnet conducting stainless steel wool is magnetized. Its surface produces the highly inhomogeneous magnetic field, namely the high gradient magnetizing field.

When the paramagnetic material passes through the steel wool in the separation tank, it is subjected to a magnetic force, which is proportional to the product of the magnetic field and the magnetic field gradient. After that, it is adsorbed on the surface of steel wool. When the non-magnetic material directly passes through the magnetic field, it through the non-magnetic material valve and pipe, and then flows into the non-magnetic product groove.

After stopping to mine, disconnect the excitation source, washing of magnetic material. Magnetic material passes through the magnetic material valve and pipe, and flow into the groove of the magnetic products.

Feature and Advantage

  1. Processing capacity is big; sorting mineral grain size range is wide; separation accuracy is high; it is suitable for rough and refined selection of medium particle weak magnetic ore.
  2. Using permanent magnetic system design of multistage separation, make it have stable work; concentrate grade can make a large quantity, and also can be guaranteed.
  3. Compact structure; small volume; light weight; it is conducive to the maintenance and overhaul.
  4. Have the advantages of large processing capacity, high efficiency, low power consumption, saving the cost.
  5. Transmission principle is simple; operation is convenient and simple; the work efficiency is very high.
  6. The mine speed is adjustable, and it can be for sorting multi-stage ore.
