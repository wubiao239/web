Flotation machine is the essential equipment in modern flotation beneficiation process. And It is currently the most effective gold separation equipment and silver separation equipment. Flotation machine is mainly used in the sorting of copper, zinc, lead, nickel, gold and other non-ferrous metals, and it can also be used for the rough election and finely selection of ferrous metals and non-metallic. Flotation machine has been the important part in the beneficiation equipment.

Working Principle

The motor drives the impeller to rotate through the belt transmission, generating centrifugal action to form negative pressure. On the one hand, it sucks in plenty of air, with the slurry mixture. On the other hand, stir the slurry, mixing with the drug. At the same time, it refines foam, making the mineral adhesive at the above of foam, then floats to the slurry surface and forms the mineralized bubbles. Adjust the gate height and control level, making useful foam be scraped.

Feature and Advantages

Full automatic control system simplifies equipment operation.

Covers area is small; the weight is light, saving investment. Under the same process capability, its covers area is only 40~60% of the mechanical agitation type.

Mineralization system, distribution system, and the flotation tank adopt the special wear-resistant material, and have the advantages of abrasion resistance and light solid, prolonging the service life of equipment.

Large processing capacity; the unit volume processing is 2~3 times of the conventional flotation machine.

Low operation cost; compared with conventional flotation machine, it saves energy of 1/3~1/2.

There is no moving part in flotation machine, and it is easy to maintain and repair.

The technological process is diversification. According to different coal, you can select roughing process or selection process, making the operation more flexible.

It has strong adaptability to raw materials.
