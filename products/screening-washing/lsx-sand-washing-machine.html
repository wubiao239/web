LSX spiral sand washing machine is a new type of sand washing equipment, which is the foundation of the traditional spiral sand washing machine. Now it is widely used in artificial sand making, electric pole, the construction site concrete dam, sand classification and dehydration of industry. It is more suitable for building sand and road construction gravel, and can wash, dehydrate and grade of the fine-grained and coarse-grained materials. This is also the main function of LSX sand washing machine.

Working Principle

LSX spiral sand washing machine is inclined to layout. At the bottom of the tank, there is a flow tank surrounded by the tri-side board. The head of screw merge is in the flow tank. The motor drives the screw to rotate by the reducer. The water is fed from the perforated plate at the bottom of flow tank.

The material enters the spiral sand washing machine. Spiral sand washing device, transports the material to the wheel sand washing machine for two cleaning while cleaning, in order to optimize the cleaning step in the sand stone production process. The sand stone cleaning is more thorough, improving the sand quality, providing a strong guarantee for high-quality aggregate.

Feature and Advantages

  1. High efficiency and energy saving; it is less energy saving 50% than traditional sand making machine;
  2. Large quantity, small power consumption, high cleanliness;
  3. The advantages of simple and reasonable structure, and convenient repair;
  4. Low loss of wearing parts, long service life, saving cost;
  5. Wide application, and the larger role;
  6. Good cleaning and dewatering effect, fine product stability;
  7. Between bearing and water, materials, is separated, to avoid the machine damage phenomenon due to rust contamination prematurely.
