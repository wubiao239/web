Vibrating Screen

Vibrating screen is the equipment with multi-layer number and high efficiency. It is suitable for sieving stone in the quarry, also can be used for product classification for coal, mining, mineral processing, building materials, electric power and chemical industry etc. It can serve a lot of material, such as granite, basalt, diabase, shale, quartz stone, gravel, limestone, iron ore, coal gangue, non-ferrous metal ore, pebbles. The feed size ranges 200mm to 400mm. the production capacity is 7.5-700T/H.

Structure

Vibrating screen is mainly made up of screen box, screen mesh, vibrator, damping spring device, chassis and other components.

Working Principle

The motor drives the exciter to rotate at a high speed through the triangle belt. The vibration sieve boxes do the circular motion with a definite amplitude. The material arises from continuous casting movement by the sieve box pass impulse. In the meet process of the material and the screen surface, the material that less than sieve particles, will pass through the sieve, so as to realize the classification.

Feature and Advantage

  1. The ring groove rivet connection has the advantages of advanced structure, firm and durable, flexible connection, stable operation.
  2. This series vibrating screen is circular vibrating screen; the material screen drip lines are long; the screening efficiency of multi-screening is high, and wide application.
  3. Have the advantages of simple structure and convenient repair.
  4. The structure is advanced; using eccentric block as an exciting force, has the advantages of excitation power, low power consumption, and low noise.
  5. The structure is small amplitude, high frequency, and large inclination, so this machine is high efficiency and large handling capacity.
  6. The vibrator adopts large clearance bearing and thin oil lubrication, so the noise is low, and the service life is long.
  7. The components have strong versatility, so the repair is convenient.
