YKN Vibrating Screen

YKN vibrating screen is my company developed new products in 20 years of experience. It is widely used in metal mines, engineering construction, coal, cement, sand and stone field, dry mortar, power plant desulfurization, solid waste treatment and other fields. It is suitable for screening ore with large block size and large bulk density, such as ferrous metal ore, non-ferrous metal ore, cobber, limestone, dolomite, granite, diabase, basalt. Its max feeding size is below 400mm, and production capacity is 100~850T/H.

Working Principle

YKN series vibrating screen is consist of the motor drive part, side plate spring seat, intermediate shaft parts, vibration components, screen mesh, etc. This series vibrating screen eccentric block as excitation source is used to drive the circular vibrating screen box to rotate. Motor is driven by triangle with intermediate shaft, and intermediate shaft drives the spindle to rotate. The spindle rotates to drive an eccentric block to rotate, and inertia force of the eccentric block force the vibration sieve box to vibrate. With the motor running, the body moves like a circle, to realize continuous screening operation.

Feature and Advantages

    The use is flexible, and the repair is convenient.

    Its application is wide, and can be used in a number of areas, to meet the needs of more customers.

    Use the external eccentric block structure exciter; intermediate transmission is connected by a flexible connection, and the vibration is stable.

    The through ability is strong; screening efficiency is high, service life is longer.

    The whole equipment torque transmission is no axial force transmission, and running is more smoothly.

    The exciter is directly fixed on the sieve box side; the connection between the two vibration exciter is through a universal coupling, so the installation and maintenance are convenient.
