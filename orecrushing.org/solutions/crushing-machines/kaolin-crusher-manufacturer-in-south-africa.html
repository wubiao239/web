      Kaolinite Crushing Plant

      Kaolin crusher is a kind of important crushing equipment to break the big mined kaolin stone into small particle size. Kaolin crusher is also called kaolinite crusher. Usually crushing process includes primary crushing and secondary crushing, sometimes also include tertitary crushing. In the kaolin primary crushing process, often use the jaw crusher or hammer crusher as primary crushing machine, It can crush kaolin into small pieces with the size less than 25.4mm. According to your needing capacity per day, you can choose which crusher as primary crusher. If you don't want to install kaolin grinding mill, you can choose tertiary kaolin crusher to crush kaolin particle thirdly. The final size can be reached 2mm in diameter.
